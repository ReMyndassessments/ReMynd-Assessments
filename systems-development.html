Student Support Systems Development

Whether you are already clear as to what your school’s needs may be, or simply have questions,  we urge you to contact us to see how we can help to implement or improve your student support identification, monitoring and reporting system. We can provide insight and information prior to setting up an actual on-site appointment for consultation and needs assessment.

Our school systems consultation and assessment process helps us determine your school’s strengths and challenges in various student support areas, thus enabling us to create a “road map” of how to proceed and ultimately build a school-specific tool. We also find it extremely helpful to observe a school's daily routine as it relates to addressing student support needs so we better understand how to encourage high levels of staff motivation and cooperation. Classroom consultations can be incorporated into this process as needed.
